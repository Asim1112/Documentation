## ğŸŒ Understanding Marketplace Models  
### 1ï¸âƒ£ What is **E-Commerce**?  
**E-Commerce** (Electronic Commerce) refers to the online buying and selling of goods and services. It includes product catalogs, order fulfillment, payment processing, and shipment tracking.  
**Examples:** Amazon, Daraz, eBay  

### 2ï¸âƒ£ What is **Q-Commerce**?  
**Q-Commerce** (Quick Commerce) is a fast-paced marketplace model where goods are delivered within **minutes to an hour**. It focuses on localized delivery and streamlined inventory management.  
**Examples:** Foodpanda, Grofers, Blinkit  

### 3ï¸âƒ£ What is **Rental E-Commerce**?  
**Rental E-Commerce** allows users to rent goods and services instead of purchasing them. It is popular in industries like real estate, vehicles, fashion, and electronics.  
**Examples:** Airbnb, Turo, Rent the Runway

## ğŸ“– Key Business Jargon to Know  
Understanding industry-specific terminology is crucial in marketplace development. Here are some key terms:  

- **SKU (Stock Keeping Unit):** A unique identifier for each product.  
- **Inventory Management:** The process of tracking and managing stock levels.  
- **Order Fulfillment:** The process of receiving, processing, and delivering orders.  
- **Logistics:** Managing the movement of goods from seller to buyer.  
- **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of visitors who make a purchase.  
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** The cost to acquire a new customer.
